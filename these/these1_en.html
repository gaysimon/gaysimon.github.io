The next step of the project consisted to give to the agent the ability to learn the notion of space. We first needed a continuous environment to keep the distances consistent regardless of the orientation of the agent. I thus developed a new environment based on the one we used before, Vacuum. This new environment allows movements in continuous space, and a cylindrical rendering system can be used to simulate every kind of sensors.
