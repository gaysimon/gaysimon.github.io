A simplified Space Memory.


The algorithms of the space memory are unfortunately too heavy to be tested simultaneously. They however shown that it is possible to learn by experience the notion of space. Based on this hypothesis, we developed a simplified version of the space memory. Objects are represented as points and segments on which we attribute their speed vectors, supposed measured by the sensory system. The agent can then associate interactions, or sequences of interactions, to these elements. A demonstration of this mechanism is available on the Ernest blog.
