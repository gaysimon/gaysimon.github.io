A Force Feedback haptic Tablet (F2T): second prototype


Coming soon...

Stronger, more reliable, more precise, this second prototype is an important improvement of the first one. We significantly improved the haptic effects and developed a more versatile interface allowing to create and edit tactile environments, and also audio-tactile environments. As this work is under publication, the different models and softwares will be available soon.
